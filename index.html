Our Story

Who We Are

Manchioneal New Testament Church of God is a Spirit-filled congregation located in the heart of Manchioneal, Portland, Jamaica. We are a community of believers committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and building strong, faith-filled lives. Rooted in the power of the Holy Spirit, we strive to be a transformative presence in our community — welcoming all who seek hope, healing, and a deeper relationship with God.

"To be a beacon of hope in Manchioneal, transforming lives through the power of Jesus Christ and the ministry of the Holy Spirit."
